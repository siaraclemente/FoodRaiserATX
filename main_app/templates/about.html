Who We Are


FoodRaiserATX helps food permitted business locations give thousands of meals each month, feeding hundreds of our neighbors living with food insecurity - the 1 in 7 people in Austin who do not know where their next meal will come from. In addition, we are starting to put a dent in the approximately 30 percent of food in the U.S. that ends up in a landfill and produces methane gas.
